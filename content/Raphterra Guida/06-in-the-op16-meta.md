# Blue Green Luffy in the OP16 Meta

> [IMMAGINE: manga art banner showing Shanks (left, red hair), Luffy (center, screaming/shocked expression), and Boa Hancock (right) — colored One Piece manga panel]

**Blue-Green Luffy** is the premier Green deck in the OP16 meta. As a Green specialist, this is the deck I chose as my main for the format. It is an incredibly balanced deck with an excellent matchup spread across the top tiers. From my experience, the OP16 format primarily revolves around six decks: **Blue-Green Luffy**, **Purple Enel**, **Blue-Yellow Nami**, **Purple-Yellow Rosinante**, **Black-Yellow Teach**, and **Red-Blue Lucy**.

## Matchup Spread

- **Favored:** **Blue-Yellow Nami**, **Black-Yellow Teach**, **Red-Blue Lucy**
- **Even:** The Mirror Match, **Purple-Yellow Rosinante**
- **Unfavored (but winnable):** Purple Enel

This matchup spread makes **Blue-Green Luffy** a fantastic competitive pick. We hold a strong advantage against most top-tier decks, and even our unfavored matchup, Purple **Enel**, struggles heavily against **Black-Yellow Teach** and **Blue-Yellow Nami**. If you can outskill or outplay your opponents, you have a very high chance of tournament success.

Outside of the top tiers, we also boast several favorable matchups in the mid-tiers, including **Red Shanks**, **Green Mihawk**, and **Purple Sengoku**.

While we do have some unfavored matchups against **Yellow Kalgara**, **Red-Green Luffy**, and **Black Yamato**, these decks are relatively uncommon. Furthermore, they tend to suffer from consistency issues, often causing them to lose to other top-tier decks in the earlier rounds of a tournament.
