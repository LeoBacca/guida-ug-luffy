## The Leader: Blue Green Luffy (OP16-022)

> [IMMAGINE: banner illustrativo — Monkey D. Luffy (art anime) che sorride di fronte a due grandi occhi verdi sullo sfondo.]

> [IMMAGINE: carta Leader "SAMPLE" — Monkey.D.Luffy, power 5000, LIFE 4, tipo Impel Down/Straw Hat Crew, codice OP16-022. Testo: Activate:Main / Once Per Turn — "If the only Characters on your field are [Impel Down] type Characters, set up to 2 of your DON!! cards as active."]

Blue-Green Luffy (often called Impel Down Luffy) is an aggressive swarm deck built around wide boards and relentless offensive pressure.

**Key Characteristics**

- **Efficient Early-Game Swarm:** Thanks to a Leader ability that restands 2 DON!! every turn, this deck excels at swarming the board early. Low-cost characters like **1C Luffy**, **2C Bon Clay**, and **2C Daz Bones** replace themselves by drawing a card on play, maintaining your hand size while threatening continuous 6k attacks.
- **Resilient Board Rebuilds:** Even if an opponent clears your board, the deck can recover instantly. Most units replace themselves upon entering the field, and mid-game swarm tools like **5C Buggy** and **6C Prisoners of Impel Down** allow you to rebuild your entire presence in a single turn.
- **Overwhelming Offensive Pressure:** A constant barrage of 6k attacks in the early to mid-game is incredibly difficult for most decks to handle. This early pressure perfectly sets up devastating late-game combo finishers. The most common finisher utilizes **1C Let's Go! To the Navy Headquarters**, which restands your entire board to push for up to 12 attacks in a single turn.
- **Dual-Color Control Tools:** Access to Blue and Green gives the deck robust defensive and control options. You can utilize stall tools like **4C Galdino** or **1C All Out** to freeze your opponent's attacks, or pivot to removal using **7C Gravity Blade** to bottom-deck major threats.
- **Typing Restrictions & Flexibility:** Despite being largely restricted to playing "Impel Down" characters, the sheer aggression and swarm potential of Blue-Green Luffy are enough to overpower a wide variety of strategies, especially mid-tier decks that fail to stabilize against early pressure.

**Turn Flexibility**

Having 2 extra DON!! to work with every turn opens up strategic flexibility. Depending on the matchup and board state, you have multiple lines of play available each turn:

- **Swarm the Board:** Deploy multiple characters simultaneously to overwhelm the opponent.
- **Play Events:** Control of your opponent's characters to stall their gameplan.
- **Hybrid Development:** Combine both development and control tools seamlessly in the same turn.
- **Maximize Attack Power:** Attach all available DON!! to characters to execute lethal finishing combos.
