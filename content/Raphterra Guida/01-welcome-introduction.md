## Why Play Blue-Green Luffy?

The OP16 format has taken the meta by storm, creating a "jungle" environment where multiple distinct decks can realistically contend for top cuts. Blue-Green Luffy is the set's dedicated aggro deck, designed to navigate this wild field with ease. It completely dismantles any strategy that cannot handle raw aggression, while maintaining highly resilient lines of play against the control decks meant to counter it.
