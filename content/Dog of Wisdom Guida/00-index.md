## Riferimento rapido — il mazzo

- **Leader:** Monkey D. Luffy (UG / Blue-Green), a.k.a. *Impel Down Luffy*.
- **Identità:** aggro a valore sostenuto; va largo, cicla, ri-erige 2 DON!! ogni turno col Leader.
- **Win condition principali:** Boa Hancock (9k Unblockable) + **Let's Go! To the Navy Headquarters!** (re-stand Event per lethal).
- **Core staple:** Luffy×4, Mr.1×4, Mr.2×4, Ivankov×4, Crocodile×4, Mr.3 Galdino×4, Buggy×5(×4), Prisoner of Impel Down×12, Boa Hancock×3, You Can Be My Samurai×4, Let's Go to the Navy HQ×2, Gum-Gum Rain×1.
